What is Base Camp Coding Academy?

Base Camp Coding Academy is a high sponsored and high paced 12 month applied software program offered through Northwest Mississippi Community College you can read about /here/.

Per their website:

"In the year-long program, students work with real world technologies to learn the fundamentals of coding, app development, project management, and the life leadership skills they will need to be successful in their career and competitive in the software development job market upon graduation. The curriculum is fast paced, focused, and aligned to the needs of employers in the modern technology environment. Currently, our technical curriculum covers Python, Django, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, SQL (PostgreSQL), Spring, and more while using editors such as Visual Studio Code, IntelliJ, and PgAdmin."
